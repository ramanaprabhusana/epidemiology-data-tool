- **No unauthorized disclosure:** Do not share the project, deliverables, or PharmaACE/InsightACE-related context (including platform details, metrics, or indication-specific data) with third parties unless permitted under the NDA or with explicit approval from PharmaACE.
- **Data and outputs:** Treat all indication-specific epidemiology data, source mappings, and scenario choices as confidential. When sharing with PharmaACE, use approved channels and avoid public repositories or unsecured storage unless otherwise agreed.
